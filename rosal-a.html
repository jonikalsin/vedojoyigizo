Rosala Biography

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Grammy winner Rosalia Vila Tobella is better known with her first name Rosalia. The Spanish singer and songwriter gained her hype from the second album for which she won her first Grammy Award and became the first Spanish to won it. She worked along with J Balvin on for which she won MTV Europe Music Awards. She has 6.01 million subscribers on her YouTube channel . 

Born And Raised Barcelona, Catalonia

Rosalia was born on 25th September 1993, in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain with name Rosalia Vila Tobella. She holds the Spanish nationality and belongs to mixed ethnicity.

She is one of two kids of Jose Manuel Vila (father) and Pilar Tobella (mother). She was along with her sister Pilar Villa Tobella in Catalonia. 

She began her musical career at the age of 16 at the Taller de Musicis in Spain. She studies at the Raval School and later was transferred to the Catalonia College of Music and completed her studies.  

At The Age of 15, She Auditioned For Show Tu Si Que Valesa

Rosalia auditioned for a role in television show but was not selected for the role. She worked with Juan Gomez at the Panama International Film Festival in 2013. She worked on various jobs including flamenco teachers and perform on musical bars and weddings.

In 2016, she collaborated with Spanish rapper C. Tangana and released the song in 2016. The song became a sleeper hit and also include in Spanish Single Chart in 2018. In 2016, she along with Raul Refree started working on duet album . Which eventually released on 10th February 2017.
