Gas Monkey Garage Cast and Dallas Location

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, May 17, 2024

The cast of Gas Monkey Garage are Richard Rawlings, Aaron Kaufman, Mike Coy, and Christie Brimberry. Richard Rawlings is the owner of the garage.

Richard Rawlings

Born on March 1969 in Fort Worth, Texas, USA, Richard is an American entrepreneur, media face, producer, and, actor. He runs the Garage.

The star of the reality TV show 'Fast N' Loud' apart from the garage, owns Gas Monkey Bar N'Grill and Gas Monkey Live Music in Dallas, Texas. The ventures are now rebranded.

As a child, Rawlings often went to car shows with his father, which enhanced his interest in this field. Eventually, in 2002 he founded the shop. 


 
The manager of the garage joined the YouTube channel on June 27, 2012, where there are 549 videos.

Throughout the Fast N' Loud show, the proprietor had several clashes with his employees. Anyone who had a tiff with him was seen quitting the show such as Aaron Kaufman, Tom Smith, and Jordan Butler.

Aaron Kaufman

Aaron Kaufman is an American TV star, racing driver, and owner of Arclight Fabrication. He is a former lead mechanic for the garage. 

He worked at a local garage in Dallas before joining Monkey Garage. Richard invited him to join the team after being impressed with his tearing and redesigning skills.

He was considered the main center of the show because of the talent he had for remodeling a car torn down to pieces.

He left in 2017, stating personal reasons.

After the split, Aaron owned his shop, Arclight Fabrication in Dalla, Texas. He also returned to TV with two shows, Shifting Gears with Aaron Kaufman and Aaron Needs a Job. 

Mike Coy 

Mike Coy was the painter for Fast N' Loud on discovery channel. He is the owner and painter at Tex Mex Motors.

He earned a lot of fans with his amazing spray paint gun skills. Born in Mckinney, Mike was keen on automobiles since childhood and did certain jobs in the related field during his teenage.

Mike Coy worked with Richard for over a decade before leaving in 2017 after a few miserable clashes with producers.

His work is appreciated by celebrities also. His talent with the brushes can be seen in the ultimate result of the car.

Christie Brimberry

Christie Brimberry worked in the garage's office, both in real and reel life. She kept a record of accounts and helped Rawlings in growing the business.

Purchasing cars, new parts, making sales, and making payments to the staff are some of her duties. Brimberry appeared in 55 episodes out of 100 episodes from 2012 to 2017.

Her husband was Richard's hairstylist. Richard in need of an administrator, approached her and hired her.
 
Christie from the garage shop has a tattoo on her hand, which add to the picture of the garage as a home for hot rodders.
