Miley Cyrus Is Sick With Tonsilitis

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, April 27, 2024

Miley Cyrus is sick with tonsilitis, the singer announced on her official Instagram account. After making headlines this weekend for spending time with friend Cody Simpson, Miley shared several photos to her Instagram where she revealed she has tonsilitis.

Miley was seen in photos with barely any makeup and lying underneath a white sheet in bed. She shared several photos that gave a close-up view of her face and though she stated she wasn’t feeling well, the Grammy-winning singer looked beautiful.

Now that the world knows that Miley and Cody Simpson were kissing, fans are wondering who gave the sickness to whom and if Cody is feeling under the weather as well.

It’s likely that Miley Cyrus is currently contagious and both she and Cody would probably do well to get a checkup at the doctor since she is sick.

Miley has been the subject of headline news since she split with her husband of less than a year, Liam Hemsworth. Miley was spotted galavanting around Italy with Kaitlynn Carter and the two were virtually inseparable for several months.

Now that relationship is over and it seems Miley has moved on with Cody Simpson. In fact, Cody seems to be so smitten with Miley, he wrote a message to her on his Prince Neptune Instagram account in the form of a sexual-themed poem.

Some have even suggested that the rapid succession of these relationships is erratic and some think there may be something else going on. More than one comment has surfaced questioning if Miley may be bipolar or having some sort of mental health issue.

Miley has announced that she knows what she is doing and is an adult. Though she may be moving quickly from one relationship to the next, her body may be telling her that it’s time to slow down, especially now that she has tonsilitis.
