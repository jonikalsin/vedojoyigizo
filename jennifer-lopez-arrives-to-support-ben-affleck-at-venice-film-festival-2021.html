Jennifer Lopez Arrives to Support Ben Affleck at Venice Film Festival 2021

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Jennifer Lopez is here to support!

The “Let’s Get Loud” superstar was spotted making a fashionable arrival on Friday (September 10) at the 2021 Venice Film Festival in Venice, Italy.
