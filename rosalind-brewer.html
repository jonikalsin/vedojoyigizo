Rosalind Brewer Biography and Wiki

Rosalind Brewer is the CEO of Walgreens Boots Alliance in the United States. She is one of only two Black female CEOs of Fortune 500 companies. Brewer is the first African American woman to be named CEO of Walgreens Boots Alliance, Starbucks Group President and COO, and Sam’s Club.

Rosalind Brewer Education

Brewer graduated from Cass Technical High School in Detroit, Michigan, in 1980, and attended Spelman College in Atlanta, Georgia, where she earned a bachelor’s degree in chemistry. Brewer also holds degrees from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business/Stanford Law School’s Director’s College and The Wharton School’s Advanced Management Program.
