What is Austin ISD Portal?

Austin ISD is the abbreviation of Austin Independent School District. America’s Austin city in Texas is where it is located.

Established in 1881, AISD provides its services to the majority of towns that surround Austin as well as Austin itself. Cities like the City of Sunset Valley, the City of San Leanna, and certain regions in Travis country also enjoy the advantages of AISD.

All 129 schools fall under the control of AISD. Of these schools, 19 are middle schools. 17 high schools are included and the remaining are elementary schools.
