How much NIL Money Did Bijan Robinson Get?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Bijan Robinson, a former Texas running back and a highly touted prospect in the upcoming NFL draft, has not only showcased his skills on the field but also made significant strides in the Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) market. This article delves into the various NIL deals and endorsements that Robinson secured during his college career, shedding light on the impressive amount of money he accumulated.

Bijan Robinson's NIL Valuation

According to On3's projected annual NIL valuation, Bijan Robinson's market value stands at an impressive $1.7 million. This valuation places him as the eighth highest-valued athlete in the NIL market. Robinson's total followers on social media, which amount to 249,000, have played a significant role in his NIL success.

Notable NIL Deals and Endorsements

Throughout his college career, Bijan Robinson secured several notable NIL deals and endorsements that contributed to his financial success. One prominent endorsement was his partnership with Lamborghini Austin, a luxury car brand. This collaboration not only provided Robinson with financial compensation but also increased his visibility and brand recognition.

Another significant deal for Robinson was the creation of his own mustard brand called Bijan Mustardson. This entrepreneurial endeavor not only showcased Robinson's creativity but also allowed him to capitalize on his personal brand and popularity.

Additionally, Robinson became an official outfitter for Rhoback, a clothing brand. This partnership not only showcased his fashion sense but also provided him with financial benefits and exposure in the fashion industry.

Influence on Draft Decision

Heisman Trophy winner Robert Griffin III has suggested that the University of Texas should offer Bijan Robinson a significant NIL deal to encourage him to stay in college instead of entering the NFL Draft. Griffin believes that the financial opportunities and exposure from NIL deals could outweigh the risk of injury in college football. However, it is ultimately up to Robinson to weigh the potential financial benefits of staying in college against the opportunity to pursue his professional football career in the NFL.

Bijan Robinson's NIL success is a testament to his talent, marketability, and entrepreneurial spirit. With an impressive projected annual NIL valuation of $1.7 million, Robinson has secured notable deals and endorsements, including partnerships with Lamborghini Austin, his own mustard brand, and an official outfitter position with Rhoback. While the influence of NIL deals on his draft decision remains to be seen, Robinson's ability to leverage his name, image, and likeness has undoubtedly provided him with significant financial opportunities during his college career.
