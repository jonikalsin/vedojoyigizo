Meet John McEnroe sons Kevin McEnroe and Sean McEnroe

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

John McEnroe is an American former professional tennis player, known for his shot-making and volleying skills, his rivalries with Bjorn Borg and Jimmy Connors, and his confrontational on-court behavior, which frequently landed him in trouble with umpires and tennis authorities.

John has been married twice. He was first married to Academy Award winner Tatum O’Neal, the daughter of actor Ryan O’Neal, in 1986. They had three children – two sons and a daughter before having divorced in 1994.

The focus of this article dives a bit into the life of his sons, Kevin and Sean. Continue reading for more information about them.

Who are John McEnroe sons Kevin McEnroe and Sean McEnroe, and what do they do?

Kevin McEnroe, the first son of John and Tatum, was born on May 23, 1986, in Los Angeles, however, he was raised in New York. Kevin is an author and a novelist by profession. He graduated from Columbia University with an MFA. Kevin McEnroe authored his first novel ‘Our Town’ in 2015. The book is inspired by the troubled life of his grandmother, actress Joanna Moore. He credits her and calls his grandmother his ‘guardian angel.’

Sean is the second son of John and Tatum. He was born on September 23, 1987, exactly sixteen months after his brother was born. In the year 2015, Sean changed his last name from McEnroe to O’Neal, however, he claimed it was nothing against his dad but a means to have his own path. Sean got married to a beautiful lady called Niamh in September 2020.
