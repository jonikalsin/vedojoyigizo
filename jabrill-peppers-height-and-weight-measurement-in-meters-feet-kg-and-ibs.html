Jabrill Peppers height and weight Measurement in meters, feet, KG and Ibs

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 16, 2024

Jabrill Ahmad Peppers is an American football strong safety for the New England Patriots of the National Football League (NFL).

He attended Don Bosco Preparatory High School in Ramsey, New Jersey. As a freshman, he started as a cornerback before also taking over as a running back as a sophomore.

He left Don Bosco to attend Paramus Catholic High School in Paramus, New Jersey. There, he was named Player of the Year by MSG Varsity after his junior season.

Peppers made a verbal commitment to play college football at the University of Michigan for the Michigan Wolverines. After two successful seasons of his collegiate career, he decided to forgo his last two seasons in favor of the 2017 NFL Draft.

In the first round (25th overall) of the 2017 NFL Draft, he was drafted by the Cleveland Browns.

Height in meters and feet

He stands at 6 ft 2 in using the imperial system, or 1.88 m, using the metric system.

Weight in KG and ibs

He weighs 209 lb using the imperial system, or 95 kg, using the metric system.
