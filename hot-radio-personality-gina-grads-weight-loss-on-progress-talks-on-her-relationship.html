How old is Gina Grad? Her Bio & Wiki

Gina Grad was born on 1st May 1978, making her 39 years of age currently. She looks very young for her age thanks to all the weight loss efforts she has put in. She was born and raised in Overland Park, Kansas having attended the University of Kansas. She is best known for being the host of the radio program “Andy and Gina in the Morning” as well as “KWSD 100.3 the Sound’ on weekdays.

She also sometimes substitutes as a host of ‘The Adam Carolla Show’. Aside from that, she is also known for her voice-over career the most famous one of which is her voice as Rose in game Street Fighter 4. She has also voiced over for other games like Syndicate and Technolyze.
