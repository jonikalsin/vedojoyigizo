Gabriela Gonzalez is an American Instagram star, surfer, TikTok star, YouTuber, and social media influencer. Gabriela Gonzalez primarily posts pictures and videos from her everyday life and travels on her Instagram page.

Early Life

Gabriela Gonzalez first opened her eyes on 6th September 2001. She was born in Florida, United States of America, and was raised alongside a younger brother named Sam, an older brother named Alex, and a sister named Rachel. She is 22 years old currently and her zodiac sign is Virgo. Her mother’s name is Lisa Brawner Gonzalez.

Moreover, she studied at Lake Mary High School, where she was part of the soccer program. Later, she attended the Orlando Surf Expo in 2015 where she met the guys from the company Roark.

She is extremely well-traveled. Her love for surfing and the sea has taken her to Australia, England, Ireland, New York, and California. However, she is a skilled surfer and even posted a picture of herself with an all-time great professional Kelly Slater to her Instagram account. Her family and friends make regular appearances in her content.

Gabriela Gonzalez – Rise to Fame

Gabriela Gonzalez has been surfing since she was very young. She has also traveled extensively throughout her life. She has been to places such as Dublin, Ireland; London, England; New York City, New York; Queensland, Australia; Los Angeles, California; Miami, Florida; and Newark, New Jersey.

Moreover. she uses TikTok very conventionally. She often makes use of Snapchat filters and puts on unique dresses to dance and lip-sync to popular songs. Further, in March 2016, she posted her first picture on Instagram. It was presumably taken from the airplane window as she arrived in Australia.

Her content on Instagram vastly differs from what she posts on TikTok. It reflects her life in Florida, her experiences during her travels, and her deep love for surfing. Gonzalez gets to interact with various types of marine life because of her surfing.

In April 2016, she posted a picture of herself with a wrasse on her Instagram. It was taken during her trip to Australia. Moreover, she took photos with Jack Avery as seen on both her and Avery’s respective Instagram accounts.

YouTube career

She joined YouTube on June 20th, 2017. To date, her oldest video was posted on May 18th, 2018. After a long break, during which she was hiding her pregnancy with an adorable Lavvy, she posted again on June 4th, 2019. Her return video was her labor and delivery story.

One of Gabbie’s main goals in 2020 is to get the channel on a routine, and she does this by posting videos every week. She is also featured on other channels as well including YOURFRIENDANDRE; Yasmin Switzer; SparklyFamilyVlogs; Anna Seavey; Allie Brooke; Maddie Lambert; and Famous Birthdays.

The most popular video on her channel is ‘visiting her dad on tour! // Europe Vlog’, with more than 1 million views. Moreover, she has either endorsed or promoted a couple of brands through social media which include the likes of MVMT, Get Back Necklaces, Vans Girls, Beginning Boutique, BLACKBOUGHSWIM, and several others.

Gabriela Gonzalez – Personal Life

In 2018, Gabriela started dating Jack Avery, a singer-songwriter presently affiliated with the five-piece boy band ‘Why Don’t We’. He is a native of Susquehanna, Pennsylvania and besides being a singer, is a prodigious piano and guitar player.

Jack was friends with his mom’s best friend’s daughter, through which he was introduced to Gabbie, who was one of her friends. Before meeting in person, he DM’d her. He finally got to see her in Los Angeles. They publicly revealed their relationship in February 2018. The couple is blessed with children of theirs. Therefore, on April 22, 2019, she gave birth to their daughter, Lavender May Avery.
