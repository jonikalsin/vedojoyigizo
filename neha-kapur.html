Neha Kapur is an Indian model and actress who rose to prominence after winning a national beauty pageant in her home country of India. Similarly, she represented India in the Miss Universe pageant in 2006. The model also drew notoriety as the wife of Kunal Nayyar, the world’s third highest-paid TV actor at one point. She has also created a successful career in the realm of fashion and design, having worked with some of the greatest names in the Indian fashion business.

Early Years, Biography, and Family

Neha Kapur was born on March 31, 1984, in New Dehli, a district of India’s capital city of Delhi. She is from a Punjabi Hindu household. Kapur is of South Asian heritage and possesses Indian nationality. Her father is from Punjab, and her mother is from Bihar. Kapur grew up in New Delhi, India, with her two older brothers. Neha attended the Springdales School in Dhaula Kuan, New Delhi, for her education. She is also an accomplished classical dancer. She also spent four years studying the Bharatanatyam style of Indian classical dance. Similarly, she spent eight years studying the Kathak form of Indian classical dance. She graduated from the Pearl Academy with a degree in fashion design.

Professional Life and Career

Neha Kapur began her modeling career after signing with Elite Model Management. She competed in the Femina Miss India 2006 beauty pageant and was named Femina Miss India Universe 2006. In addition, Kapur won numerous other prominent awards in the contest, including Femina Miss Fresh Face and Femina Miss Photogenic.

Kapur represented India at the Miss Universe 2006 pageant on July 23, 2006, at the Shrine Auditorium in Los Angeles, California, after winning the Femina Miss India Universe 2006 crown. She made it to the semifinal round of the competition, where she was chosen as one of the top 20 contestants. Kapur’s modeling career grew in popularity and success as a result of her successful outings. She went on to grace the covers of various well-known Indian magazines, including Elle, Femina, Man’s World, India Today, L’Officiel, and Travel Plus.

Relationships, Marriage, and Husband

She is a married woman of Indian origin. She married Kunal Nayyar in a small wedding ceremony in December 2011. Nayyar, her spouse, is a British Indian actor and comedian. He rose to prominence after appearing in the highly anticipated TV series The Big Bang Theory alongside Johnny Galecki, Jim Parsons, Kaley Cuoco, and Simon Helberg.

The couple met for the first time in 2008. After three years of dating, they exchanged vows. The couple is without a child. They now live in Los Angeles, California.
