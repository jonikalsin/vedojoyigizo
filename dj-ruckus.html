Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Ruckus is a deejay. Ruckus was a resident performer at the Hakkasan nightclub located in Las Vegas. The remarkable DJ has also played at events held by designers like Louis Vuitton and Alexander Wang among others.

Ruckus has likewise been called “the most sought-after DJ in the world” by GQ Magazine.

Talking about the beginning of his career in the world of music, DJ received his 1st set of turntables as one of his 14th birthday gifts from his parents. After only 2 years, i.e. at the age of 16 years, DJ was spinning at clubs across Miami.

Moreover, DJ once deejayed a bar mitzvah for one of the sons of director Steven Spielberg. Steven is a notable film director of films like E.T., Jaws, and Jurassic Park among others.

Steven also received the Academy Award for Best Director for the films Schindler’s List and Saving Private Ryan. Moreover, Steven is also the director of the action/adventure classic trilogy Indiana Jones which stars Harrison Ford.

More Details

At the age of 15 years, Ruckus became the resident DJ at Crobar in Miami Beach. The young DJ’s exciting mix of hip-hop and urban hits soon brought him opportunities to fly to the coasts for gigs at New York’s Lotus and Los Angeles’ Shelter.

Then in the year 2004, Janet Jackson’s song from the album Damita Jo, “Sexhibition” introduced DJ Ruckus as a producer.

The young DJ remixed and co-produced tracks by popular personalities including Ice Cube, Macy Gray, OutKast, and TLC during the day.

Ruckus spent his nights DJing parties held by celebrities like Diddy, Christina Aguilera, Adrien Brody, and Steven Tyler. The DJ also appeared in the film ATL in the year 2006. With this project, DJ added acting to his résumé.

Furthermore, Ruckus is also popular as the celebrity cousin of Zoë Kravitz. Zoë is a remarkable actress, model, and singer. She is also famous as the celebrity daughter of Lenny Kravitz and Lisa Bonet.

Lenny is a musician and actor and Lisa is also an actress. Talking about DJ’s cousin Zoë, she made her acting debut with No Reservations in 2007.

The actress’s breakthrough was as the character, Angel Salvadore, of X-Men: First Class in 2011.
